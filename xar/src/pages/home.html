Welcome to MOM-CA

Monasterium.net is the largest virtual archive for medieval and early-modern charters in Europe. The Collaborative Archive (MOM-CA) provides scholars, archivists, and students with tools to browse, edit, and publish charter descriptions following the CEI standard.
