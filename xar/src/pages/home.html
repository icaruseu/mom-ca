Welcome to the Collaborative Archive

The Collaborative Archive (MOM-CA) provides scholars, archivists, and students with tools to browse, edit, and publish charter descriptions following the CEI standard. With contributions from archives across Europe, Monasterium.net offers unprecedented access to medieval documentary heritage.

Whether you are researching a specific charter, exploring the holdings of an archive, or contributing your own transcriptions, MOM-CA provides the tools you need.
